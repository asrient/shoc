Welcome!
We thank you for your interest in Sen house of children. Our school has served families in Salt Lake since 1974 when it was founded by the Sen family. We are proud to be a parent participation preschool.
As parents, we are all continually searching for the perfect school for our children. We hope that the information you will find here will guide you in your decision.

Our goal is to instill a joy in learning that each child will carry throughout their life.
SHOC offers a home-away-from-home atmosphere that makes children feel comfortable and helps them explore freely. Within this safe environment we provide a developmental curriculum. This means that planning is done through a process of observing the children, evaluating their individual interests and needs, then planning the activities accordingly.
